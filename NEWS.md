# tptest 1.0.0

* Initial CRAN release.
* Implements the Sasabuchi (1980) joint slope test for U-shaped and
  inverse U-shaped relationships.
* Supports quadratic, cubic, log-quadratic, and inverse functional forms.
* Delta-method standard errors and confidence intervals for the turning point.
* Optional Fieller interval and Simonsohn (2018) two-lines robustness test.
